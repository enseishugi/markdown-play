# Emphasis
1. For emphasis, both *this* and _this_ work.
1. For bold, both **this** and __this__ work.
1. Both can be combined like __*this*__.

# Lists
## Unordered lists
Start every node with a * character:
* Like
* This
  * And
  * Subtrees
    * Are
    * Supported

## Ordered lists
Start every node with a 1. string:
1. Like
1. This
   1. And
   1. Subtrees
      1. Are
      1. Supported
